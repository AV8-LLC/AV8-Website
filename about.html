About Nathan Nance

I’ve spent decades working in professional audio, most notably as an assistant re-recording mixer at Skywalker Sound. My work has included film trailers, commercials, and high-end Atmos mixing for major projects. That experience taught me how to hear detail, shape emotion through sound, and deliver clarity even in complex environments.

Over the years I’ve learned that great sound isn’t just about expensive gear — it’s about understanding the space, the people in it, and what they’re trying to communicate. That perspective carries directly into my work with churches.

I believe clear sound matters deeply in worship. When the message can be heard clearly and the music supports the moment instead of fighting it, something meaningful happens. That’s why I’m passionate about helping churches — not because it’s easy work, but because I genuinely believe it’s important.

I’m not above any job. Whether it’s tuning a small sanctuary system, helping design a larger install, or simply giving honest advice about what you actually need, I bring the same care and attention I’ve used on major film projects. My goal is to serve churches well with practical, high-quality solutions that fit their space and their budget.

If you’re looking for someone who understands both the technical side and the heart behind why good sound matters in church, I’d be honored to help.
