About Nathan Nance

I’ve had the privilege of working as a re-recording mixer at Skywalker Sound on major films and television projects. My work includes films for Disney, Pixar, and Lucasfilm, as well as acclaimed series for Netflix. Credits include Mank (Academy Award-nominated for Best Sound), Toy Story 4, Incredibles 2, and Mindhunter.

That experience taught me how sound shapes emotion and how critical clarity is when people are trying to connect with something meaningful. Over time I realized those same principles matter deeply in churches. When the sound is clear, the message lands. When it’s muddy or harsh, people disengage.

I’m not here to sell the biggest system possible. I’m here to help churches get the right system for their space and their budget — and to make sure it actually works the way it should. I bring the same care and standards I used on major projects to every job, whether it’s a small sanctuary or a larger install.

At the end of the day, I’m a husband and a dad who believes that clear sound in worship spaces is genuinely important. I’m not above any job, and I’m happy to help however I can — whether that’s designing a full system, tuning what you already have, or training volunteers so your team can run sound with confidence.

If you’re looking for someone who understands both the technical side and the heart behind why this work matters, I’d be honored to serve your church.
