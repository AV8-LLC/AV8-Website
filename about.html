About Nathan Nance

I’ve spent decades working in professional audio, including years as an assistant re-recording mixer at Skywalker Sound. My experience includes film trailers, commercials, and high-end Atmos mixing. That work taught me how sound shapes emotion and how critical clarity is when people are trying to connect with something meaningful.

Over time I realized that the same principles that matter in film mixing — detail, intention, and emotional impact — matter just as much in churches. When the sound is clear, the message lands. When it’s muddy or harsh, people disengage. I’ve seen the difference good audio makes, and I believe it’s worth doing well.

I’m not here to sell the biggest system possible. I’m here to help churches get the right system for their space and their budget — and to make sure it actually works the way it should. I bring the same care and standards I used on major projects to every job, whether it’s a small sanctuary or a larger install.

At the end of the day, I’m a husband and a dad who believes that clear sound in worship spaces is genuinely important. I’m not above any job, and I’m happy to help however I can — whether that’s designing a full system, tuning what you already have, or just giving honest advice.

If you’re looking for someone who understands both the technical side and the heart behind why this work matters, I’d be honored to serve your church.
